What Are Meme Coins?

Meme coins are cryptocurrencies born out of internet memes, pop culture phenomena, and social media virality. Unlike traditional blockchain assets designed for complex utility, meme coins rely heavily on community engagement, viral marketing, and collective speculation.

Risk Disclaimer: Meme coins are hyper-volatile assets driven by market sentiment. Always practice strict risk management and never invest funds you cannot afford to lose completely.
