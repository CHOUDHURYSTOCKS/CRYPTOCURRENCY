💰 Cryptocurrency Trading & Investment Guide

Learn essential crypto terms in a simple and beautiful way

Basic Crypto Terms

  • Cryptocurrency: Digital money secured by cryptography.
  • Bitcoin (BTC): The first and most popular cryptocurrency.
  • Ethereum (ETH): Blockchain supporting smart contracts.
  • Altcoin: Any crypto other than Bitcoin.
  • Stablecoin: Crypto tied to stable assets like USD.

Trading Terms

  • Bull Market: Prices are rising.
  • Bear Market: Prices are falling.
  • Long Position: Expecting price to rise.
  • Short Position: Expecting price to fall.
  • Leverage: Borrowed funds (high risk).
  • Liquidity: Ease of buying/selling.
  • Volume: Total traded amount.

Price & Chart Terms

  • Candlestick Chart: Shows price movement visually.
  • Support Level: Price where fall may stop.
  • Resistance Level: Price where rise may stop.
  • Trend: Market direction.

Investment Terms

  • HODL: Hold long-term.
  • DCA: Invest fixed amounts regularly.
  • Portfolio: Your crypto collection.
  • Diversification: Spread investments.
  • ROI: Profit/loss percentage.

Blockchain & Technical

  • Blockchain: Decentralized ledger.
  • Wallet: Stores crypto.
  • Private Key: Secret access key.
  • Public Key: Address to receive funds.
  • Gas Fees: Transaction fees.

Risk & Strategy

  • Volatility: Price fluctuations.
  • FOMO: Buying due to hype.
  • FUD: Negative sentiment.
  • Stop-Loss: Limit losses.
  • Take-Profit: Lock gains.

Advanced Terms

  • DeFi: Finance without banks.
  • Staking: Earn rewards.
  • Yield Farming: Maximize returns.
  • NFT: Unique digital assets.
  • Market Cap: Total value.

🚀 Quick Beginner Tips

  • Focus on Bitcoin & Ethereum
  • Use DCA strategy
  • Avoid high leverage early
  • Learn risk management first
