Learn About Cryptocurrency

Cryptocurrency is a digital or virtual form of currency that uses cryptography for security. It operates independently of a central bank and has a decentralized structure.

Crypto History Timeline

2008 - Bitcoin Concept

Satoshi Nakamoto publishes the Bitcoin whitepaper introducing blockchain-based digital currency.

2009 - Bitcoin Launch

The first Bitcoin software is released and the genesis block is mined.

2011 - Altcoins Rise

Alternative cryptocurrencies like Litecoin and Namecoin emerge to improve upon Bitcoin's technology.

2015 - Ethereum & Smart Contracts

Ethereum is launched, enabling smart contracts and decentralized applications (dApps).

2017 - ICO Boom

Initial Coin Offerings (ICOs) gain popularity as a fundraising method for crypto projects.

2021 - NFT Craze

Non-fungible tokens (NFTs) become mainstream, representing digital art and collectibles on blockchains.
