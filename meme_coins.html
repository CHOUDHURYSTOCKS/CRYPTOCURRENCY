✅ Basic Screening Criteria

  • Market Cap: $100K–$5M
  • Liquidity: $50K+
  • 24H Volume: $100K+
  • 500+ Holders
  • 3–7 Days Trading History
  • Active Telegram/X/Discord
  • No obvious contract risks

🚩 Red Flags

  • Liquidity under $20K
  • Large creator ownership
  • Very few holders
  • Fake trading volume
  • Honeypot contracts
  • Removable liquidity
  • Anonymous team
  • No real community

⚠ Risk Management

  • Risk only 1–2% of total capital per trade.
  • Always use stop-loss.
  • Take partial profits.
  • Avoid buying based solely on hype.

Daily Routine

  1. Check DexScreener new pairs.
  2. Filter liquidity above $50K.
  3. Sort by trading volume.
  4. Check holder count.
  5. Verify contract with RugCheck or GoPlus.
  6. Review X (Twitter) & Telegram.
  7. Create watchlist.
  8. Wait for confirmation.
Disclaimer

Meme coins are highly speculative cryptocurrencies. Their prices are driven primarily by community sentiment, social media activity, speculation, and liquidity rather than fundamental utility. Many newly launched meme coins fail within days or weeks, and manipulation is common. Always perform independent research and never invest money you cannot afford to lose.
