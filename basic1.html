Cryptocurrency Trading and Investment Terms

Basic Crypto Terms

  • Cryptocurrency: Digital or virtual money secured by cryptography.
  • Bitcoin (BTC): The first and most popular cryptocurrency.
  • Ethereum (ETH): A blockchain that supports smart contracts and apps.
  • Altcoin: Any crypto other than Bitcoin.
  • Stablecoin: Crypto tied to stable assets like USD.

Trading Terms

  • Bull Market: Prices are rising; investors are optimistic.
  • Bear Market: Prices are falling; investors are pessimistic.
  • Long Position: Buying expecting price to go up.
  • Short Position: Betting price will go down.
  • Leverage: Borrowed funds to amplify trades.
  • Liquidity: Ease of buying/selling assets.
  • Volume: Total amount traded.

Price & Chart Terms

  • Candlestick Chart: Shows price movement visually.
  • Support Level: Price where demand may stop a fall.
  • Resistance Level: Price where selling may stop a rise.
  • Trend: Direction of the market.

Investment Terms

  • HODL: Hold long-term regardless of price swings.
  • DCA: Investing fixed amounts regularly.
  • Portfolio: Collection of assets.
  • Diversification: Spreading investments.
  • ROI: Profit or loss percentage.

Blockchain & Technical Terms

  • Blockchain: Decentralized ledger.
  • Wallet: Stores crypto (hot/cold).
  • Private Key: Secret access key.
  • Public Key: Address to receive funds.
  • Gas Fees: Transaction fees.

Risk & Strategy Terms

  • Volatility: Price fluctuations.
  • FOMO: Buying due to hype.
  • FUD: Negative sentiment.
  • Stop-Loss: Limit losses automatically.
  • Take-Profit: Lock in gains.

Advanced Terms

  • DeFi: Financial services without banks.
  • Staking: Earn rewards by holding crypto.
  • Yield Farming: Maximize returns across platforms.
  • NFT: Unique digital asset.
  • Market Cap: Total crypto value.

Quick Beginner Tip

  • Focus on Bitcoin & Ethereum
  • Use DCA strategy
  • Avoid high leverage early
  • Learn risk management first
